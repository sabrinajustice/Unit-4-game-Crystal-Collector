Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

Each of the four crystals below will add a certain amount of points to your total score.

You win the game if your total score matches the random number, and lose if your total score goes above it.

The value of each crystal changes after game end. This value is random from 1 to 12 and is hidden from you.
